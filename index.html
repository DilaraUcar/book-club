Do you love reading and want to be part of a book-club?

Our Story

Welcome to Love Reading club, in a local cafe in Stockholm a small group of passionate readers came together with a shared dream. In 2003 our journey began with a love for books and a vision to create a community where the joy of reading is celebrated. With the desire to connect with fellow book enthusiasts, discuss compelling stories, and broaden our literary horizons, Love Reading club was founded.

Why join us and love reading together?

Benefits

There are many benefits as to why reading is good for you. Here are just some of the reasons: improves your focus, memory, empathy, and communication skills. It can reduce stress, improve your mental health, it allows you to learn new things to help you succeed in work and relationships.

Encourage

Reading requires free time, and it can be difficult to carve out time each day or week to sit down with a good book. Being a part of our book club, incentivizes you to pick up that book. And with accountability partners it will make it a more easier and fun journey.

Social

Meeting new people who share a common interest creates a sense of belonging, and because you have this common interest conversation comes easy. You can make new friends or expand your circle.

Learn

Reading is one of the best ways to acquire knowledge. Participating in our book club means you’ll gain insight from the books themselves but also through discussion with other people in the group.
